The streets of Bangkok. What is there to do? A lot, surely.

[Source](https://www.reddit.com/r/ThailandTourism/comments/1bb8dac/what_are_some_must_domust_visitmust_try_things_in/?rdt=54224) Must-Visit Places in Bangkok
- Grand Palace & Wat Phra Kaew – Iconic temple complex.
- Wat Arun – Beautiful temple by the river, great for sunset views.
- Wat Pho – Famous for the Reclining Buddha and Thai massages.
- Chatuchak Weekend Market – Huge outdoor market for shopping.
- Jim Thompson House – Traditional Thai architecture and silk museum.
- Chinatown (Yaowarat Road) – Bustling street food and shops.
- MBK Center/Siam Paragon – Popular malls for shopping.
Must-Do Activities
- Boat Tour on Chao Phraya River – See key landmarks from the water.
- Visit Floating Markets – Like Damnoen Saduak or Amphawa.
- Muay Thai Match – Watch Thailand’s national sport live.
- Thai Cooking Class – Learn to cook traditional dishes.
- Traditional Thai Massage – Famous therapeutic experience.
- Rooftop Bars – Enjoy skyline views (e.g., Sky Bar, Vertigo).
- Get a Bespoke Suit – Bangkok is f famous for tailor-made suits.
Must-Try
- Food: Pad Thai, Tom Yum Goong, Som Tum, Mango Sticky Rice, Khao Pad, Street Food
- Nightlife: Asiatique The Riverfront, Khao San Road, Soi Cowboy
- Cultural Experiences: Thai Dance Shows, Explore the Khlongs
